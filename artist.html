Manuel Perfection de Really Man

Real Name: Anyovi Emmanuel

Biography

Born on February 19, 2000, Manuel Perfection — real name Anyovi Emmanuel — began his music career as a church singer at the age of 12.

In 2018, he joined the Hidden Talentz Comedy Crew in Moyo, where he developed both his performance and musical artistry.

He released his first official song, "Akapela", on July 24, 2021. Since then, he has grown steadily in the Ugandan music scene.

His latest audio release, "Ma Baba" featuring Justine Chris, dropped on February 28, 2025. It’s a powerful Afrobeat love song with cultural, dancehall, and gospel influences — showcasing his unique blend of styles and readiness to rise.
